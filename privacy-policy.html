Privacy Policy

Compliance and Data Protection

At WorkNest, we are committed to ensuring full compliance with all applicable laws and regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). We prioritize the protection of your data and privacy, implementing industry-standard security measures to safeguard your personal information.

Data Collection and Usage

We collect, store, and process personal information strictly in accordance with legal requirements and ethical standards. Our data-handling practices are regularly reviewed to maintain compliance with evolving regulations and industry best practices.

Transparency and Accountability

Transparency is at the core of our operations. We clearly outline how your data is collected, used, and stored. Our policies reflect our ongoing efforts to maintain compliance and accountability.

User Rights and Choices

As part of our commitment to compliance, we respect your rights to access, update, and delete your personal information. You can request data modifications or removals by contacting us at worknest@gmail.com.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal details such as name, email, phone number, business details, and payment information when you book an office space or sign up for our services.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Process office space bookings
  • Send booking confirmations and updates
  • Provide customer support and assistance
  • Improve our services and offerings
  • Send promotional emails (if opted-in)

Data Retention Policy

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy. Data will be securely deleted when no longer needed.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your data to third parties. However, we may share limited information with trusted partners (e.g., payment processors) to provide essential services.

Security Measures

Your data is protected with encryption, firewalls, and secure servers to prevent unauthorized access, data breaches, or misuse.

Payment Security

All payment transactions are handled through secure payment gateways that comply with PCI-DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard).

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and analytics tools to improve your experience. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Marketing and Communications

If you opt-in to our newsletter, we may send promotional content. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in our emails.
